  title: 'Computational Framework for Human–AI Symbiosis: A Multidimensional Approach to Augmented Intelligence and Mutual
    Learning'
  authors: Shravanchandra G, T. Ananth Kumar, Vinayagan S, Vishnu Kumar Mishra, Megha Mishra, K. Parijatha
  abstract: Human-AI symbiosis focuses on bid irectional augmentation where humans and AI systems co-adapt for improved joint
    performance. Despite conceptual advances, the field lacks a generalizable computational architecture, rigorous mutual-learning
    algorithms, and standardized benchmarks for evaluating joint human-AI outcomes. We propose a multidimensional computational
    framework that integrates an explicit human cognitive model, online mutual-learning algorithms, an interaction manager
    for adaptive explainability and trust calibration, and privacy-preserving federated update mechanisms. We describe an
    evaluation plan with benchmark tasks, metrics for joint gains and fairness of benefit, and both short-term and longitudinal
    user studies. The proposed work aims to provide a reproducible foundation for research and deployment of symbiotic Human-AI
    systems.
