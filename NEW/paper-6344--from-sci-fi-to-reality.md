  abstract: The vision of a world interconnected by 6G IoT applications is now closer than ever. As we approach the deployment
    of 6G networks, the need for secure, efficient, and scalable solutions becomes increasingly critical. This chapter addresses
    the challenges and technological innovations that are transforming science fiction into tangible 6G IoT solutions. The
    chapter focuses on the key technologies driving the development of 6G IoT, with an emphasis on advanced security solutions
    essential for protecting large networks of connected devices. As 6G IoT is expected to link billions of devices across
    various applications, from autonomous vehicles to healthcare systems, securing these devices and the data they produce
    is vital. Traditional security models are no longer enough, and more advanced methods, like quantum-safe cryptography
    and decentralized trust models such as blockchain, are essential for ensuring data integrity and privacy. Additionally,
    the chapter highlights the role of AI and machine learning (ML) in enhancing the security and performance of 6G IoT applications.
    These technologies enable real-time monitoring, threat detection, and autonomous decision-making, ensuring that security
    systems can adapt to new risks as they emerge. The chapter concludes with a roadmap for integrating these technologies
    into practical, secure 6G IoT systems, with an emphasis on ongoing research, pilot projects, and international cooperation.
    Through the strategic use of cutting-edge technologies, this chapter demonstrates how the once speculative ideas surrounding
    6G IoT are being transformed into secure, scalable, and efficient solutions, paving the way for a new era of connectivity.
