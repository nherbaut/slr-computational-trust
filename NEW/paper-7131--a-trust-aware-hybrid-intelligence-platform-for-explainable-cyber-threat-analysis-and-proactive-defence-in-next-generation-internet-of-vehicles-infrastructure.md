  title: A Trust-Aware Hybrid Intelligence Platform for Explainable Cyber Threat Analysis and Proactive Defence in Next-Generation
    Internet of Vehicles Infrastructure
  authors: Bethi Saiteja, V. Sudha Rani
  abstract: Internet of Vehicles (IoV) has become one of such disruptive technologies which allows to transform the transportation
    system by connecting vehicles, infrastructure, and cloud-based services. Nevertheless, with the growth of the complexity
    of the IoV systems, as well as their interconnection, the latter become more susceptible to cyber threats. Existing security
    measures are inadequate particularly explainability and proactive defence. The paper presents a trust-based hybrid intelligence
    platform that aims to improve the cyber threat analysis and the cyber threat defence infrastructures in the next-generation
    IoV infrastructures. The platform integrates machine learning methods with trust models to deliver explanation of cyber
    threats, which is transparent and usable. Experimental analysis on a real world IoV dataset indicates an enhancement of
    12% in the accuracy of threat detection and a 15 percent decrease in false positives as compared to that of the traditional
    defence models. The proposed platform provides a dynamic and defendable defence strategy with explainable features capable
    of responding to changes in threats and enhancing security of IoV infrastructure to a great extent.
