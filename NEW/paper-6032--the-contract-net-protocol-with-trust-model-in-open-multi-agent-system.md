  title: The Contract Net Protocol with Trust Model in Open Multi-Agent System
  authors: Xiang Zhao, Houkuan Huang, Lijian He
  abstract: As one of the most important coordination mechanisms, contract net protocol (CNP) has been extensively studied
    in many fields, where many other techniques are also used to improve the decision quality. CNP suffers from multiple disadvantageous
    limitations in the open environment of MAS, so the trust model, a fundamental issue in large-scale open distributed systems,
    is introduced into CNP in this paper. Based on reviewing CNP and the trust model of the initiator side, we add the trust
    model, with direct trust and recommendation-based trust components, into CNP process. Experimental results show that the
    initiators with trust model almost steadily get the best participants no matter the environment is honest-dominated or
    dishonest-dominated. Even in the dishonest-dominated environment, this approach gets better results and is proved valuable.
