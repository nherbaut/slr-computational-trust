  title: 'Designing Novel Trust Models: Merging Psychological Insights with Machine Learning to Enhance AI User Trust'
  authors: Shirin Abdallah Alimour, Jamal Hassan Abu-Attiyeh, Munther Balawi
  abstract: Trust is one of the crucial elements of human-AI interaction. As user-friendly AI technologies are increasingly
    becoming part of everyday life, it is necessary to establish trust among users for better adoption. This paper argues
    for a conceptual-level integration of psychological trust theories—cognitive, emotional, and behavioral dimensions—with
    Machine learning (ML) processes to improve trust in AI systems. We propose a model grounded in the alignment of transparency,
    reliability, adaptability, and AI ethics with cognitive and emotional trust mechanisms that continuously construct, regulate,
    and maintain user trust in the context of human-AI interactions. This kind of framework presents a model way to enhance
    explainability, responsiveness, and fairness in AI, such that user trust scales smoothly with advancements in AI technology.
