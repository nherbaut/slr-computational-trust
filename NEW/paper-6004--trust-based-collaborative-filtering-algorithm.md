  title: Trust -Based Collaborative Filtering Algorithm
  authors: Xiaowei Xu, Fudong Wang
  abstract: The rapid development of internet has brought us into the era of information explosion, which brings the widespread
    application of the personalized recommendation system. Collaborative filtering recommendation algorithm is the most widely
    used algorithms in the personalized recommended system, but it faces problems like the data sparsity, cold start," idler"
    attack. with the development of social network, many e-commerce sites, social network sites introduce the trust mechanism,
    which becomes the new approach to overcome the problems of the traditional collaborative filtering algorithms. This paper
    first introduces the collaborative filtering recommendation algorithm and the existing problems, and then summarizes the
    current trust based collaborative filtering algorithms. for the trust based collaborative filtering algorithms, this paper
    summarizes the trust expressions and metrics, and focus on analyzing the representative trust models.
