  title: A ZTNA-Based Architecture for Secure and Trusted Access in Campus Networks
  authors: Qi Liu, Ying Zhu
  abstract: This paper presents a Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA) system for university campus networks, designed to overcome
    the limitations of traditional perimeter security. The architecture integrates with the campus mobile ecosystem, employing
    a dynamic trust scoring model powered by machine learning (XGBoost) to enable real-time, context-aware authorization.

    study demonstrates ZTNA as a practical solution for securing campus digital services.
